Welcome to the most depressing game ever made!
  • Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to try to escape the prison you are trapped in. You must open a door you can never go through. How tragic :(
  • To move, first tap on a Helicopter to focus on it, then tap anywhere that has a clear line of sight to the selected Helicopter.
  • The currently selected Helicopter is the only orange spinning Helicopter.
  • Handles are the little squares or circles connected by dotted lines to the knobs on Doors or Portals.
  • Keys are hollow circles or squres that can be picked up by Helicopters in and are used to power doors and portals.
  • Doors and Portals only open if and only if all the Handles connected to them have either a Key or a Helicopter to power it.
  • A circular Key cannot power a square Handle, and a square Key cannot power a circular Handle.
  • Portals are the pulsing circles. Open portals are purple, closed portals are red.
  • Portals are only open if and only if exactly two Portals are fully powered by their connected Handles.
